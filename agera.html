The Agera

Power Without Limits

The Regera marks a major evolution in Koenigsegg’s philosophy, moving from traditional hypercar engineering into a hybrid era of performance and refinement. Before it, the Agera lineage defined the brand’s identity with raw combustion power and extreme speed. The Agera, followed by the more powerful Agera R, refined the formula with increased performance and flexibility, while the Agera RS pushed the limits even further, becoming one of the fastest production cars in the world with record-breaking top speed achievements.

Building on this legacy, the Regera introduces a completely different approach with its hybrid Direct Drive system, focusing on effortless acceleration, instant torque delivery, and grand touring comfort while still maintaining Koenigsegg-level performance.
