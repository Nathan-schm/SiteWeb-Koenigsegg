Founded by Christian von Koenigsegg, the Swedish brand Koenigsegg Automotive has redefined the limits of performance and innovation in the hypercar industry. From a young age, Christian von Koenigsegg pursued a vision of creating the ultimate supercar, combining cutting-edge technology with uncompromising design.

Established in 1994, Koenigsegg quickly gained recognition for its engineering excellence and unique approach to automotive development. Every vehicle is handcrafted in Sweden and features groundbreaking technologies, from advanced aerodynamics to revolutionary powertrains.

Today, Koenigsegg stands as one of the most exclusive and innovative car manufacturers in the world, continuously pushing the boundaries of speed, performance, and engineering while staying true to its original vision.
