The jesko

spirit of performance

The Jesko is named after Jesko von Koenigsegg, the father of Christian von Koenigsegg. It features a redesigned 5.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine producing up to 1280 hp on standard gasoline and 1600 hp on E85 biofuel, paired with the innovative 9-speed Koenigsegg Light Speed Transmission (LST). With advanced aerodynamics generating up to 1400 kg of downforce, the Jesko is built to dominate the track.
The Jesko lineup includes two versions: the track-focused Jesko Attack and the high-speed Jesko Absolut. The Absolut holds the record for extreme speed performance, while the Attack is renowned for its aggressive design and track capabilities.
