The One:1

Power-to-Weight Ratio

The Koenigsegg One:1 is a landmark in automotive engineering, designed around a revolutionary concept: achieving a perfect 1:1 power-to-weight ratio, where one megawatt of power equals one metric ton of mass.

Powered by a 5.0L twin-turbo V8 engine producing 1,360 horsepower, the One:1 was one of the first production cars to reach the magical 1 megawatt output. Every aspect of the car was engineered for extreme performance, from its lightweight carbon fiber chassis to its advanced aerodynamics capable of generating immense downforce at high speeds.

Unlike traditional hypercars balancing comfort and usability, the One:1 was built with a singular focus: maximum performance on both road and track. Its brutal acceleration, extreme top speed, and race-derived technology earned it the title of the world’s first “Megacar,” redefining the limits of what a road-legal vehicle can achieve.

Today, the One:1 remains one of the most iconic and extreme Koenigsegg creations ever built, representing a pure expression of speed, engineering precision, and innovation.
