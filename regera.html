The Regera

Beyond Transmission

The Regera represents a radical shift in Koenigsegg’s engineering philosophy, moving away from traditional hypercar design toward a new era of hybrid performance and luxury. Unlike conventional high-performance vehicles, it eliminates the gearbox entirely thanks to Koenigsegg’s innovative Direct Drive system, delivering seamless and instantaneous acceleration.

Powered by a twin-turbo V8 engine combined with three electric motors, the Regera produces a massive 1500 horsepower and over 2000 Nm of torque. This unique setup allows for brutal acceleration while maintaining a smooth and refined driving experience, making it as comfortable as it is powerful.

While previous models like the Agera, Agera R, and Agera RS focused on raw combustion performance and record-breaking speed, the Regera introduces a new approach, prioritizing instant torque, efficiency, and grand touring comfort without compromising performance.

With only 80 units produced, the Regera stands as one of Koenigsegg’s most exclusive and innovative creations, blending cutting-edge technology with luxury and extreme performance.
