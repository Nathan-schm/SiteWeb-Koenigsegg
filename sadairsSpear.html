The Sadair's Spear

Built for the Track

The Sadair’s Spear represents the most extreme evolution of Koenigsegg’s track-focused engineering. Based on the Jesko Attack, this limited-production model pushes performance even further with increased power, reduced weight, and a sharper aerodynamic profile designed for maximum circuit efficiency.

Powered by a twin-turbo V8 engine producing up to 1625 horsepower on E85 fuel, the Sadair’s Spear delivers an extraordinary power-to-weight ratio, surpassing even the legendary One:1. Every component has been optimized for performance, from enhanced cooling systems to a more aggressive aero setup generating massive downforce.

While the Jesko lineup balances track performance and high-speed capability, the Sadair’s Spear focuses purely on lap times, offering a more intense and uncompromising driving experience. With extremely limited production, it stands as one of the most exclusive and performance-oriented Koenigsegg models ever created.
